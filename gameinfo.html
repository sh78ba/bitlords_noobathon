System Shock

The System Shock remake emulated the first game to a T, which works both for and against this game. Purists and fans of the original System Shock will love just how much of the experience is preserved in a remake that looks and feels amazing to play.

The Ascent is a unique top-down shooter game from Neon Giant, packed with cyberpunk-themed action that can be experienced alone or in a co-op game mode with friends. In a futuristic world where corporations own everything, the player must fight through the sudden fall of Ascent Group to find their freedom.

The Ascent is presented from an isometric view overlooking the character(s). It is a twin-stick shooter. It includes the ability to aim both high and low on enemy targets, allowing the player to differentiate between shooting enemies closer or further away. This mechanic is key to the game's cover system, allowing the player to aim at enemies over the top of the props in the environment. The player can fully customize their character's appearance, and can augment their character with cyberware upgrades to get new skills and abilities. Players have an upgradeable cyberdeck device, which they can use to gain access or "hack" into previously locked areas and loot certain chests.

The Indent The main character: an indent working out of Cluster 13, under Stackmaster Poone. Bristling with ambition and with a taste for mindless violence.

Your IMP An AI installed in the player character's head, which is near-universal in the setting.

Stackmaster PooneThe supervisor of cluster 13. A notoriously cheap man with a crude disposition who behaves more like a local crime lord than the local supervisor for the megacorporation.

nogHead A hacker operating out of the abandoned Black Lake district near Cluster 13, nogHead is first encountered when working for Poone but continues to play an important role in the plot from there.
