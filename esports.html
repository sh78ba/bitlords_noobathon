Citizen Sleeper

Citizen Sleeper uses the principles of traditional role-playing games with a relaxing soundtrack and meditative setting to create an immersive adventure. Players take on the role of an enslaved robot on the lam, hiding out on a seedy space station from the lawless types who have put a bounty on their heads and the bounty hunters who are eager to collect the reward.

Gunpoint

Gunpoint is a stealth-focused puzzle platform game in which players play the part of Richard Conway, a futuristic freelance spy tasked with breaking into 2D buildings on behalf of various clients. In Gunpoint, players take on the dual cyberpunk roles of hacker and spy.

VA-11 Hall-A: Cyberpunk Bartender Action

Not only is VA-11 Hall-A Cyberpunk Action one of the best pixel cyberpunk games, but it also possesses a captivating story that is packed nicely into a visual novel. While working as a bartender at the futuristic bar lovingly nicknamed Valhalla, players will learn the story of this cyberpunk city through its patriots. However, unlike the usual multiple choices for a player to choose from when discussing these.

Stray

Players have continued to be interested in dystopian cities in recent years because. This adventure game in which players take on the role of a cat trying to climb back to the surface also reveals a robot-run world. With no idea where any of the human people have gone, players are left to theorize why this city exists below the surface, away from the sunlight they have seen.

Katana Zero

Action-packed and fast-paced games continue to be the most popular genre of gaming for the theme of cyberpunk. Katana Zero is a game with pixel graphics that challenges players to go fast and avoid danger while traversing another cyberpunk world. In this 2D platformer, players take on the role of Subject Zero, an amnesiac skilled with psychic powers.

Deus Ex

Released in the early 2000s, Deus Ex is an iconic classic of the cyberpunk genre that many players have praised for being a great role-playing game as well. Set in the year 2052, players take on the role of JC Denton, a non-augmented rookie of the United Nations Anti-Terrorist Coalition.
